Let :math:`\theta=\cos^{-1}x`. So, :math:`T_n(x)=\cos n\theta`. Now,
:math:`T_{n+1}(x)=\cos(n+1)\theta`. and
:math:`T_{n-1}(x)=\cos(n-1)\theta`. Consider
:math:`T_{n+1}(x)-2xT_n(x)+T_{n-1}(x)`\ after solving we get=
:math:`2\cos n\theta \cos\theta-2x \cos n\theta`\
:math:`= 2x\cos n\theta -2x \cos n\theta = 0`

now we will replace :math:`x_n` in :math:`V_{n+1}` with :math:`x`.

Hence :math:`V_{n+1}` now becomes the function of :math:`x`.

let
:math:`P \left({x}\right) = \begin{vmatrix} 1 & x_0 & x_0^2 & \cdots & x_0^{n-2} & x_0^{n-1} & x_0^n \\ 1 & x_1 & x_1^2 & \cdots & x_1^{n-2} & x_1^{n-1} & x_1^n \\ 1 & x_2 & x_2^2 & \cdots & x_2^{n-2} & x_2^{n-1} & x_2^n \\ 1 & x_3 & x_3^2 & \cdots & x_3^{n-2} & x_3^{n-1} & x_3^n\\ \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots & \vdots & \vdots \\ 1 & x_{n-1} & x_{n-1}^2 & \cdots & x_{n-1}^{n-2} & x_{n-1}^{n-1}& x_{n-1}^{n} \\ 1 & x & x^2 & \cdots & x^{n-2} & x^{n-1} & x^n \end{vmatrix}`.

now Perform row expansion by last row.

Then :math:`P \left({x}\right)` is seen to be a polynomial of degree
:math:`n`:

::math:`P \left({x}\right) = \begin{vmatrix} x_0 & x_0^2 & \cdots & x_0^{n-2} & x_0^{n-1} & x_0^n \\ x_1 & x_1^2 & \cdots & x_1^{n-2} & x_1^{n-1} & x_1^n \\ x_2 & x_2^2 & \cdots & x_2^{n-2} & x_2^{n-1} & x_2^n \\ x_3 & x_3^2 & \cdots & x_3^{n-2} & x_3^{n-1} & x_3^n \\ \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \ & \vdots \\ x_{n-1} & x_{n-1}^2 & \cdots & x_{n-1}^{n-2} & x_{n-1}^{n-1}& x_{n-1}^{n} \\ \end{vmatrix} + \begin{vmatrix} 1 & x_0^2 & \cdots & x_0^{n-2} & x_0^{n-1} & x_0^n \\ 1 & x_1^2 & \cdots & x_1^{n-2} & x_1^{n-1} & x_1^n \\ 1 & x_2^2 & \cdots & x_2^{n-2} & x_2^{n-1} & x_2^n \\ 1 & x_3^2 & \cdots & x_3^{n-2} & x_3^{n-1} & x_3^n \\ \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \ & \vdots \\ 1 & x_{n-1}^2 & \cdots & x_{n-1}^{n-2} & x_{n-1}^{n-1} & x_{n-1}^{n} \\ \end{vmatrix}x \ \ + \ \ \cdots \ \ + \ \

We can see that statement :math:`P \left({x}\right) = 0` holds true for
all :math:`x_1, x_2, \cdots x_{n}` because if
:math:`x=x_1, x_2, \cdots x_{n}` starting determinant would have two
equal rows so :math:`V_{n+1} = 0`.

By the Polynomial Factor Theorem:
:math:`P \left({x}\right) = C \left({x - x_0}\right) \left({x - x_1}\right) \dotsm \left({x - x_{n-1} }\right)`

where :math:`C` is the leading coefficient

Thus
::math:`P \left({x}\right) = V_{n} \left({x - x_0}\right) \left({x - x_2}\right) \dotsm \left({x - x_{n-1} }\right)`
which by returning :math:`x = x_n` gives:

::math:`V_{n+1} = V_{n} \left({x_n - x_0}\right) \left({x_n - x_1}\right) \dotsm \left({x_n - x_{n-1} }\right)`

Repeating the process:

:math:`V_{n+1}=\prod_{0\mathop \le i \mathop < n} \left({x_n - x_i}\right) V_{n}`\
$ =:raw-latex:`\prod`\_{0 :raw-latex:`\mathop `:raw-latex:`\le `i
:raw-latex:`\mathop < n} \left({x_n - x_i}\right) \prod_{0\mathop \le i \mathop < n} \left({x_{n-1} - x_i}\right) V_{n}$<br>`
:math:`=...`\
:math:`=\prod_{0 \mathop \le i \mathop < j \mathop \le n} \left({x_j - x_i}\right)`

By Leibniz formula, det(V) is a polynomial in the :math:`x_i` with
integer coefficients. All entries of the ith column have the total
degree i – 1. Thus, again by Leibniz formula, all terms of the
determinant have the total degree :math:`0+1+2+...+n=\frac{n(n+1)}{2}`

if :math:`x_i = x_j` for :math:`i\neq j`, then Clearly
